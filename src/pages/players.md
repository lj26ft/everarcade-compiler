# Players

EverArcade worlds are places that remember what happened.

## Why these worlds are different

Many online games feel alive until a season resets, a server closes, or a publisher changes direction. EverArcade is designed for worlds whose history, ownership, and continuity can remain meaningful beyond one client or host.

## Persistence

A persistent world keeps going. The path you build, the market you influence, the settlement you defend, and the rivalries you create can become part of the world rather than disappearing between sessions.

## Ownership

Ownership is about more than an item icon. It is the ability for world state, history, and community value to remain portable and understandable as tools, hosts, and interfaces change.

## History

History gives a world identity. Players should be able to ask what happened, why a place matters, and how a community got here.

## Continuity

Continuity means the world keeps its story straight. Buildings can age. Economies can evolve. Cultures can form. The world remembers because its history is treated as a first-class part of the experience.
