# Trust Assumptions

## Assumptions

- SHA-256 collision resistance
- Canonical serialization correctness
- Deterministic execution
- Correct receipt ordering
- Honest external anchor publication

## Explicit Non-Assumptions

- No trusted setup
- No trusted verifier
- No trusted operator
- No proprietary cryptography

## Questions For Review

- What assumptions are missing?
- What assumptions are hidden?
- What assumptions can be removed?
