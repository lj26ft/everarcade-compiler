We’re building EverArcade around `world.evr`: portable, verifiable, persistent worlds.

A third-party independent oracle reproduced replay end-to-end over 5 production journals. Restore and migration are independently reproducible over supplied fixtures, with receipt roots derived from bundle accumulators instead of trusted stored roots.

Now we’re opening the path for Founding Worlds: teams that want to build worlds people can play, verify, contribute to, and fork.
