# What Is A World?

A world is a persistent digital place that can keep growing after a launch, season, patch, or operator change.

Worlds contain more than levels and assets. They contain communities, economies, governance, places, institutions, stories, and history.

## Persistent worlds

A persistent world remembers what happened. Players can build, trade, organize, explore, and change the shared environment over time.

## Communities

A world becomes meaningful when people gather around it. Guilds, cities, factions, marketplaces, and social norms can become part of the world's identity.

## Economies

Items, resources, services, and markets can accumulate value because the world has continuity. Players can understand scarcity and history instead of starting from a blank slate every cycle.

## Governance

Worlds can define who is allowed to change rules, publish upgrades, run infrastructure, or moderate shared spaces.

## History

History is the memory of a world. It explains why a city exists, who built a monument, how a market changed, and what a community survived.
