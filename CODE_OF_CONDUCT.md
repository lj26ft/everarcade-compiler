# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in EverArcade welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while correcting inaccurate claims clearly.
- Keep criticism focused on ideas, code, documentation, and evidence.
- Welcome new contributors and help them find canonical documentation.
- Be honest about prototype, scaffold, and production-readiness boundaries.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Misrepresenting project maturity in ways that could mislead users or operators.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, request behavior changes, limit participation, or block contributors who violate this code. Enforcement should be proportional, documented when appropriate, and focused on preserving a safe and productive project environment.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to repository maintainers through the least-public available channel. Maintainers should review reports promptly and protect reporters from retaliation.
