# Commercial Revenue Guide

The Commercial Revenue Layer v0.1 is a deterministic, replayable, non-custodial economic model. It records player-activity-derived revenue events, attribution, distribution intent, settlement intent, analytics, portal views, metrics, and replay roots without production billing or live payments.

Revenue records are ordered by epoch, event id, revenue type, origin, and beneficiary. Every event names an origin, beneficiary, revenue type, status, epoch, and distribution policy. Registry operations support create, update, and archive records.

Canonical roots are generated for the revenue registry, revenue domains, distribution, settlement intents, analytics, portal integration, metrics, economic sustainability, and replay. Replay reproduces the revenue root exactly.
