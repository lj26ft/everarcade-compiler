>
> Implementations may attach non-authoritative economic metadata, but live payments, custody, accounting, treasury services, and commercial settlement are not implemented here.

# Treasury

World treasuries fund operators, builders, rewards, and public goods. In EverArcade, treasury execution should be explicit and auditable.

A world profile should show treasury health in user language: funded, constrained, paused, or needs contributors. Detailed hashes and settlement proofs belong behind **Verify**.

Treasury policy connects to World Git because contributors need visible paths from work to review, merge, and reward.
